About Medical AI Assistant

This project is a demo of a Medical AI Assistant that can accept documents and images, extract and summarize their content, and provide health tips based on user symptoms. It uses OCR, NLP, and AI summarization models.

  • Upload medical images or PDFs for instant AI-powered explanations.
  • Check your symptoms and get basic health tips.
  • Register and login to keep your uploads private.
